Problem Statement & Project Aim

SLIDE 2
❌ The Problem
  • Manual record-keeping is error-prone and opaque
  • No real-time visibility for group members
  • Missed contributions go undetected without enforcement
  • Payout order disputes due to lack of clear rules
  • No digital audit trail or transaction history
✅ The Solution
  • Fully digital savings circle platform
  • Automated wallet deductions — no manual chasing
  • Grace period & penalty enforcement built in
  • Transparent payout rotation with pre-set order
  • Full ledger of every transaction in real-time
🔬

Core Automated Workflow

SLIDE 6
🕐

1. Cron Job Fires (Hourly / Daily)

cronJobs.js triggers both the Contribution Scheduler and Payout Scheduler at set intervals

💳

2. Auto-Debit Members' Wallets

contributionScheduler.js queries Firestore for due schedules and debits each member's wallet via a Firestore atomic transaction

⏳

3. Grace Period & Penalty Enforcement

Insufficient balance triggers a grace deadline written to Firestore. After expiry, the contribution is marked OVERDUE and the group cycle is marked DELAYED

🏦

4. Funds Pool in Group Escrow

Collected funds are transferred to the group's currentEscrowBalance in Firestore, verified before payout is released

🚀

5. Auto-Payout to Next Recipient

payoutScheduler.js verifies all contributions, deducts platform commission, and auto-credits the lump sum to the recipient's Firestore wallet

✅
